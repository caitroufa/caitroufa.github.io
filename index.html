Mission Statement

Hi! I'm Cait.

I'm a weather and physics nerd on the path to completing a PhD. My research interests include large scale climate dynamics, teleconnections, and severe weather climatology. I am particularly passionate about mentoring and educating others and in community and climate justice issues. I value hard work, tenacity, kindness, and collaboration and enjoy exploring complex problems. When I am not engaged in research, you will find me exploring the US in a 1981 VW Westfalia Vanagon with my family, taking photos of the sky, hiking or running with my dog, and listening to live music.

Bio

Currently, I am a PhD Students and GTA at Northern Illinois University, where I study atmospheric science and GIS and plan and teach weather dynamics.
